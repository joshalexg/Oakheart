Oakheart is a five piece metalcore band hailing from Glens Falls, New York. Formed in 2016 by Aaron Lawson and Tristan Marler, they quickly became a favorite within the local scene for their stage commanding presence and unique style of metal they've dubbed "Cold State Hate".

Their signature sound is driven by the groove oriented riffs from guitarists Aaron Lawson and Quintin Golonka emphisized with the rhythm section of Tristan Marler on drums and Josh O'Kelly on bass. The guitar riffs and tone which have a cold and mechanical element help to craft the signature "Cold State Hate" sound which Oakheart is known for. Songs such as "Age of Desperation" feature a surprising yet welcomed section of atomspheric and melodic guitar lines that give a sense of calm to the otherwise anger filled sound that Oakheart is known for. Their latest album "Cold State" can be heard now on all major streaming services.
